Bromo

Tenteng Wisata

Mount Bromo (Indonesian And Javanese: Gunung Bromo) Is An Active Somma Volcano Andpart Of The Tengger Mountains, In East Java, Indonesia. At 2,329 Meters (7,641 Feet) It Is Notthe Highest Peak Of The Massif, But The Most Famous. The Area Is One Of The Most Visitedtourist Destinations In East Java, And The Volcano Is Included In The Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park. The Name Bromo Comes From The Javanese Pronunciation Of Brahma, The Hindu God Of Creation. Mount Bromo Is Located In The Middle Of A Plain Called "Sea Of Sand" (Javanese: Segara Wedi Or Indonesian: Lautan Pasir), A Nature Reserve That Has Been Protected Since 1919.
